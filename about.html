Introduction

Hon'ble Shri Ajit Anantrao Pawar, popularly known as Ajitdada Pawar, was born on 22 July 1959 in Deolali Pravara, Ahmednagar District, Maharashtra. He has represented the Baramati Assembly Constituency for several years and has served in important positions in the Government of Maharashtra, including Deputy Chief Minister and Cabinet Minister.

Leadership

Ajitdada Pawar is known for his disciplined administration, quick decision-making, and commitment to public service. His leadership has focused on infrastructure development, agriculture, finance, education, irrigation, and rural development across Maharashtra.

Public Service

Throughout his political career, he has worked on improving public infrastructure, strengthening irrigation systems, supporting farmers, expanding educational facilities, and promoting industrial and economic growth in Maharashtra.

Major Areas of Work

  • Road & Infrastructure Development
  • Agriculture and Farmer Welfare
  • Irrigation & Water Conservation
  • Education Development
  • Finance & Planning
  • Industrial Growth
  • Urban & Rural Development
  • Public Welfare Initiatives
