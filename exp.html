Leadership Journey

A Journey of Leadership, Public Service and Development

Journey Through the Years

1959

Born on 22 July 1959 in Deolali Pravara, Ahmednagar District, Maharashtra.

Early Life

Completed his education and became actively involved in public service and cooperative activities.

1991

Elected as Member of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly from Baramati Constituency.

1999

Played an important role in the formation and growth of the Nationalist Congress Party (NCP).

2004

Served in important ministerial roles in Maharashtra, contributing to irrigation, water resources and infrastructure.

2010

Served as Deputy Chief Minister of Maharashtra and handled important administrative responsibilities.

2019

Continued contributing to Maharashtra's development through finance, planning and public administration.

2024

Continuing to serve Maharashtra with a focus on development, infrastructure, agriculture, education and public welfare.

"Leadership is about serving people with dedication, vision and responsibility."
