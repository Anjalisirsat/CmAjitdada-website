AJITDADA

Leadership Journey

Important Milestones in Public Service

1959

Born on 22 July 1959 in Deolali Pravara, Ahmednagar, Maharashtra.

1991

Elected as a Member of the Maharashtra Legislative Assembly from Baramati.

1990s–2000s

Served in various ministerial positions and worked in departments including Agriculture, Water Resources, and Planning.

2010

Served as Deputy Chief Minister of Maharashtra and handled important administrative responsibilities.

2019

Continued contributing to state administration and public welfare through leadership roles.

Present

Focused on infrastructure development, agriculture, education, finance, and public service in Maharashtra.
